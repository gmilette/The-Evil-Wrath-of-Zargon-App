## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Journey back to 1998 with The Evil Wrath of Zargon, a faithful Android port of the classic QBASIC RPG adventure. Explore the vast land of GEF, battle fearsome monsters, and complete an epic quest to defeat the evil Zargon.

CLASSIC RETRO RPG EXPERIENCE
Step into a nostalgic tile-based adventure that captures the spirit of late-90s PC gaming. This authentic port preserves the original game's charm while bringing smooth modern controls and performance to your Android device.

EPIC QUEST SYSTEM
Embark on a multi-stage adventure filled with mystery and discovery:
- Free the Boatman trapped under rocks
- Gather materials to build a ship
- Seek the Necromancer's forbidden powers
- Navigate treacherous rivers to reach Zargon's castle
- Face the ultimate evil in a climactic showdown

EXPLORE A VAST WORLD
The land of GEF spans 16 interconnected maps filled with secrets:
- Dense forests teeming with monsters
- Sandy deserts hiding ancient treasures
- Winding rivers and shallow waters
- Rocky mountains and mysterious graveyards
- Hidden items crucial to your quest

STRATEGIC COMBAT
Engage in turn-based battles against diverse enemies:
- Level up your character and grow stronger
- Learn powerful magic spells
- Equip better weapons and armor from shops
- Use tactics to defeat increasingly dangerous foes

KEY FEATURES
- Authentic retro graphics and gameplay
- Rich story with multiple NPCs and quests
- Character progression with stats and leveling
- Magic system with various spells
- Item collection and inventory management
- Save your progress at healing fountains
- Weapon shops to upgrade your equipment
- Strategic turn-based combat
- Multiple maps to explore
- Quest tracking and hints system

MEET MEMORABLE CHARACTERS
- Boatman: Your key to crossing the river
- Sandman: Guide who knows the land's secrets
- Necromancer: Master of life and death
- Mountain Jack: Mysterious mountain dweller
- Old Man: Keeper of wisdom

NOSTALGIA MEETS MODERN GAMING
Originally created by high school students at Snappahed Software in 1998-1999, this beloved QBASIC game has been lovingly ported to Android. Experience the same challenging gameplay and engaging story that captivated players over two decades ago, now optimized for mobile.

Perfect for fans of classic RPGs, retro gaming enthusiasts, and anyone who appreciates the golden age of PC adventure games. No ads, no in-app purchases, just pure nostalgic gaming fun.

Download now and prove you have what it takes to defeat The Evil Wrath of Zargon!
