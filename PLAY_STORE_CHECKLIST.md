## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Do I need a privacy policy if I don't collect data?**
A: Yes, Google Play requires all apps to have a privacy policy URL, even if you don't collect data.

**Q: Where should I host my privacy policy?**
A: GitHub Pages (free), your website, or Google Docs. It must be publicly accessible.

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 2-7 days, but can take up to 14 days for first-time submissions.

**Q: What if Google asks for clarification?**
A: Use the explanation provided in `DATA_SAFETY.md` under "If Google Asks for Clarification"

**Q: Can I change the privacy policy later?**
A: Yes, but you must update both the hosted version and notify users of changes.
