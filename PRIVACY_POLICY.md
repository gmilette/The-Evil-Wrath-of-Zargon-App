# Privacy Policy for Zargon

**Last Updated: December 26, 2025**

## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Zargon ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.**

The App is a single-player, offline role-playing game that stores game progress data locally on your device only.

### Local Game Save Data

The App stores the following game-related information on your device using Android's SharedPreferences:

- **Character Statistics**: Health points, magic points, experience level, attack/defense stats
- **Game Progress**: Current map location, story progression status, quest completion
- **Inventory**: Items collected during gameplay
- **Play Time**: Total time spent playing the game
- **Save Metadata**: Save slot numbers and timestamps

**Important:** This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

---

## How We Use Your Information

The locally stored game data is used solely to:
- Save your game progress
- Resume gameplay from where you left off
- Track quest and story progression
- Maintain your character's statistics and inventory

## Data Storage and Security

All game save data is stored locally on your Android device using Android's secure SharedPreferences storage mechanism. Your device's operating system protects this data from access by other applications.

**Android System Backup:**
- Your device may automatically back up app data to your Google account if you have enabled Android's backup feature in your device settings
- This is a standard Android system feature controlled by you
- We do not have access to these backups
- You can disable this feature in your Android Settings

---

## Data Deletion

You have complete control over your game save data:

**To delete all game data:**
1. Go to Android Settings
2. Navigate to Apps → Zargon
3. Select Storage
4. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"

**Alternative:** Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored game data from your device.
